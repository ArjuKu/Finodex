## 1. Valuation vs. Pricing
- **Valuation** is "What is it worth?" (The Math).
- **Pricing** is "What will it sell for?" (The Deal).

---

## 2. Method 1: IPO Pricing
When a company goes public, an investment bank helps set the price for the first day of trading.

**Steps:**
1. Look at peer multiples.
2. Calculate fair value.
3. Apply 10-15% **IPO Discount** to create a "pop" on day 1.

**Python Tool**: [Run IPO_Pricing.py](./Python/IPO_Pricing.py)

## 3. Method 2: M&A Pricing
When one company buys another, they decide on the **Purchase Price**.

```
Purchase Price = Target Equity Value + Acquisition Premium
```

## 5. Summary
Investment Banking pricing is the **art of the deal**.
1. IPOs are priced at a discount.
2. M&A deals are priced at a premium.
3. Internal budgeting keeps the company running.
